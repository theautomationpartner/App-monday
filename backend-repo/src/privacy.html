Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

1. Who we are

Factura ARCA ("the App", "we", "us") is an electronic-invoicing application for the monday.com marketplace, operated by The Automation Partner ("the Company"). The App lets monday.com users issue legally valid electronic invoices and related fiscal documents (credit notes, debit notes) through Argentina's tax authority (AFIP/ARCA) directly from their monday.com boards.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we process, why, who we share it with, and how we protect it. By installing or using the App you agree to this Policy.

2. Data we process and why

2.1 Issuer (your business) fiscal data

Legal name, trade name, tax ID (CUIT), fiscal address, phone, email, website and logo. We store this to generate the legally required electronic invoices and render the invoice PDF on your behalf.

2.2 AFIP/ARCA digital certificate and private key

Required to authenticate with AFIP's web services (WSAA) and issue invoices on your behalf. The private key is encrypted at rest using AES; the encryption key is held only as a server-side secret and is never stored alongside the data in our database.

2.3 monday.com access

To read the relevant board item and write the result back (authorization code "CAE", invoice number, status, PDF), the App uses the short-lived token that monday.com includes in each automation-recipe execution. This token is used in memory for that single request and is not persisted. We also process your monday.com account, workspace and user identifiers solely to isolate each customer's data (multi-tenant separation).

2.4 Invoice recipient (your customers') data

Tax ID / national ID (CUIT/DNI), name, fiscal condition and address of the party you are invoicing. This data is legally mandated by AFIP/ARCA to issue a valid invoice — an invoice cannot be emitted without it. Recipient information retrieved from AFIP's public taxpayer registry is cached temporarily (about 24 hours) only to reduce repeated calls to AFIP.

3. Third-party services we use

The App relies on the following third-party providers, on both the frontend and backend. We share with each provider only the data strictly necessary for the stated purpose.

ProviderWherePurpose & data involved
monday.com Frontend & backend Host platform. Reading the board item to invoice and writing the result back (CAE, PDF, status). Session/automation tokens are validated to authenticate requests.
AFIP / ARCA (afip.gob.ar web services) Backend Argentina's tax authority. The App sends invoice data (issuer, recipient, line items, amounts) to AFIP's official web services (WSAA, WSFEv1) to obtain the authorization code (CAE). This is legally required to issue the invoice.
DigitalOcean Backend Cloud infrastructure: the application server and the managed PostgreSQL database where your fiscal data and encrypted credentials are stored.
Cloudflare Frontend & backend DNS, TLS/HTTPS encryption and CDN. All traffic to and from the App passes through Cloudflare in encrypted form.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not share it with any party other than those listed above. Sharing with AFIP/ARCA is a legal requirement inherent to issuing the invoice itself.

4. How we protect your data

  • Encryption at rest: sensitive credentials (AFIP private keys) are encrypted with AES; the key lives only in the server environment, never in the database.
  • Encryption in transit: all traffic uses HTTPS/TLS (via Cloudflare); the database connection uses PostgreSQL SSL in verify-full mode with certificate pinning to DigitalOcean's certificate authority.
  • Managed, access-controlled infrastructure: hosted on DigitalOcean (application server + managed PostgreSQL cluster), not publicly exposed.
  • Request authentication: every request is authenticated by validating the session token signed by monday.com with our client secret.
  • Tenant isolation: data is scoped per account and workspace; one customer can only access its own data.
  • Security headers: HSTS, a restrictive Content-Security-Policy and MIME-sniffing protection are enforced.

5. Data retention and deletion

We retain your data only while the App is installed and for as long as needed to provide the service and comply with applicable fiscal record-keeping rules.

When you uninstall the App, your operational data is removed and personal contact information is anonymized; we retain only aggregate, non-identifying information. You may also request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.
